About The Wick's Hive

Wickerbees are soft, fluffy critters that take on their own role within the world of Sirewood. While they aren't the only creature that exists in Sirewood, they are the most prominent species that does tremendous work day to day. These creatures live, breathe, eat and create wax, otherwise typically used for candles. They are beings made from wax and magic; They are created the moment their parents put together their ignis (their life force), and create their offspring. Bees have an affinity for exploring and collecting; crafting their brews to be perfected, to possibly ascend to the likes of their Novelty royals; the variant that brings them closest to their goddess, Calliope. While Bees can come in many different shapes, sizes, forms and colors, there is specifics when it comes to their variants; specific aspects that makes them fall under a bracket to identify within a certain class. These classes typically determine the role within the hive, if the hive follows traditional values passed on through many generations. The world of Sirewood is vast, much to explore, find, and discover. Many things that can be revealed within the discoveries of the land.
